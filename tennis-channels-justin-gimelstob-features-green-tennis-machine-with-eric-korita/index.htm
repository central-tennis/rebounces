Tennis Channel’s Justin Gimelstob Features Green Tennis Machine with Eric Korita

green tennis, reBounces Clients, reBounces Updates, Testimonial, Video

Justin Gimelstob features the Green Tennis Machine on Tennis Channel

“It’s doing amazing things for the tennis industry.”

We want to again thank Justin and his crew for taking the time to do a quick interview with Eric about the Green Tennis Machine.

A full transcript of the Justin Gimelstob interview is here:

Justin Gimelstob: We’re here with Eric Korita, a former top 50 singles player. A former top 30s doubles player, but more importantly now the Vice President of Sales of a revolutionary new invention, the Green Tennis Machine. Eric what exactly does this machine do?

Eric Korita: We have created a way to put the air back in the tennis ball. It lasts, it’s saving clubs literally thousands of dollars. We are in municipal facilities, schools, colleges. We have found a way to help save a lot of money in the game.

Justin Gimelstob: Why is this so important to the tennis industry?

Eric Korita: We feel we can bring more people to the sport that way. After the clubs and the academies use the use the balls, they can actually give them away and recycle them. So it’s been a great initiative to help really grow the sport.

Justin Gimelstob: And the BNP Paribas Open has been an incredible partner to the Green Tennis Machine. Why are they a perfect fit for you guys?

Eric Korita: BNP brought us in 5 years ago. They have been, they’re wonderful globally in tennis. They are one of the largest sponsors in tennis. Their green initiative globally is massive.

Justin Gimelstob: Eric thanks a lot, it’s an incredible invention the Green Tennis Machine. It’s doing amazing things for the tennis industry. Eric thanks for your support.

Eric Korita: Thanks Justin I appreciate it.
