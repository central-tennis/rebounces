BNP Paribas and reBounces Partner to Recycle Tennis Balls at 2015 BNP Paribas Open

green tennis, reBounces Updates, Testimonial, Video

BNP Paribas Open and Rebounces Recycle Tennis Balls

2015 marked the 7th year Rebounces has partnered with BNP Paribas to recycle tennis balls collected at the BNP Paribas Open.

This year 2,000+ tournament patrons helped to keep their used tennis balls out of the landfills.

To encourage the more than 456,000 attendees of the BNP Paribas Open to help green the game of tennis, reBounces and BNP Paribas sponsored a tennis ball recycling program onsite at the event. Tennis fans were asked to bring in six old balls in exchange for a can of three new ones. At the end of the spectacular event, over a ton (seriously 2,000+ pounds) of recycled tennis balls were packed and shipped back to reBounces Head Quarters in Arkansas diverting them from an immediate trip to the landfill.  Read about past BNP Paribas Open tennis ball recycling events OR get a pre-paid shipping label to recycle tennis balls today.
